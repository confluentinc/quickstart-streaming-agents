# New Orleans Pelicans Season Opener vs Chicago Bulls

**Frequency:** Multiple times per season
**Time:** 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Smoothie King Center, Central Business District
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

NBA season opener featuring the New Orleans Pelicans hosting the Chicago Bulls in front of 16,800+ fans for the official start of the 2024-25 basketball season. Opening night festivities include pre-game ceremonies, player introductions, and fan celebration activities.

The season opener attracts passionate basketball fans from across the Gulf South region, many attending their first Pelicans game of the season. Pre-game activities begin at 5:30 PM with fan zone entertainment and promotional events in the arena concourse.

Transportation demand peaks during arrival (6:00 PM - 7:00 PM) and departure (10:00 PM - 11:00 PM) periods. The Wednesday evening timing creates demand from business professionals and families seeking convenient transportation from work locations and hotels throughout the city.

Many season opener attendees combine the game with dinner in the CBD or French Quarter, creating extended demand patterns as fans transition between dining venues and the arena throughout the evening.
