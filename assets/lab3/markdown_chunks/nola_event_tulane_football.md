# Tulane Football Home Game

**Frequency:** 6 times per season (Sep-Nov)
**Time:** 2:30 PM - 6:00 PM
**Location:** Yulman Stadium, Uptown
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

College football games featuring Tulane Green Wave at on-campus stadium. Attracts 25,000-30,000 fans including students, alumni, and local supporters for afternoon kickoff games.

Transportation demand peaks during pre-game arrival (1:00 PM - 2:15 PM) and post-game departure (6:00 PM - 7:00 PM). Fans from French Quarter hotels and downtown areas utilize riverboat service to reach Uptown campus location, extending celebrations to nearby bars.
