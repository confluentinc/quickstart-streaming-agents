# Garden District Upscale Dinner Service

**Frequency:** Daily evening service
**Time:** 6:30 PM - 9:30 PM
**Location:** Commander's Palace and neighboring fine dining
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Peak dinner service at Garden District's renowned restaurants featuring upscale Creole cuisine. Establishments accommodate 300-500 diners seeking elegant evening dining experiences in historic settings.

Transportation demand builds during dinner arrival (6:15 PM - 7:15 PM) with well-dressed diners from French Quarter hotels and CBD areas. Formal dining occasions create preference for sophisticated riverboat arrival complementing elegant atmosphere.
