# Traditional Jazz Funeral Parade Memorial - Treme

**Frequency:** Recurring cultural event
**Time:** 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
**Location:** Starting at St. Augustine Church, Treme, ending French Quarter
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Traditional New Orleans jazz funeral parade honoring beloved local musician Professor Longhair tribute artist, featuring multiple brass bands and second line parade through historic Treme neighborhood into the French Quarter. The procession celebrates life through authentic New Orleans musical traditions.

This cultural event attracts locals and visitors seeking authentic New Orleans experiences, with the parade route creating temporary street closures and generating foot traffic throughout Treme and French Quarter areas. Multiple brass bands participate including Rebirth Brass Band and Dirty Dozen Brass Band.

Transportation demand occurs along the parade route as spectators follow the procession and as participants seek transportation to join different segments of the parade. Peak demand during parade conclusion (4:30 PM - 5:30 PM) as crowds disperse from French Quarter.

The parade's cultural significance attracts music enthusiasts and cultural tourists who often extend their visit with French Quarter dining and music venue visits, creating sustained demand throughout the historic districts.
