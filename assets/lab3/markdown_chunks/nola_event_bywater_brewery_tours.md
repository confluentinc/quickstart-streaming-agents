# Bywater Brewery Tours

**Frequency:** Daily afternoon tours
**Time:** 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
**Location:** Bywater neighborhood breweries
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Craft brewery tours and taproom visits in Bywater's growing beer scene. Multiple breweries offer tours throughout afternoon, attracting beer enthusiasts and tourists exploring neighborhood's industrial-chic atmosphere.

Transportation demand builds mid-afternoon (2:00 PM - 3:00 PM) with craft beer fans from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Riverboat service provides convenient access to Bywater while avoiding parking challenges in dense neighborhood.
