# Streetcar Wine Tasting Tour

**Frequency:** Multiple weekly departures
**Time:** 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
**Location:** Multiple Uptown and Garden District wine bars
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Guided wine tasting tour utilizing historic streetcar with stops at upscale wine bars. Small groups of 12-18 wine enthusiasts explore neighborhood wine venues with expert sommelier guidance.

Light transportation demand at tour start (2:45 PM - 3:15 PM) with participants from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Specialized tour attracts affluent tourists seeking sophisticated afternoon activity combining transportation and tasting.
