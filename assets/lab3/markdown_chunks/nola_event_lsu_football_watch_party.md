# LSU Tigers Football Watch Party - Champions Square

**Frequency:** Multiple times per season
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Champions Square, Central Business District
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Massive outdoor viewing party for LSU Tigers vs Texas A&M football game featuring giant LED screens, live music, LSU cheerleaders, and authentic Louisiana food vendors. The free event accommodates 8,000+ passionate LSU fans in downtown New Orleans.

This Saturday evening event attracts LSU alumni and fans from throughout New Orleans and surrounding parishes who cannot attend the Baton Rouge game. The family-friendly atmosphere includes kids' activities, face painting, and LSU merchandise vendors.

Transportation demand peaks during arrival (5:30 PM - 6:30 PM) and departure (9:30 PM - 10:30 PM) periods as purple-and-gold clad fans seek convenient access to Champions Square. The CBD location creates demand from fans staying in French Quarter hotels and Uptown residences.

Many watch party attendees continue LSU celebrations at nearby French Quarter and CBD bars after the game, creating sustained transportation demand throughout the downtown entertainment district for post-game festivities.
