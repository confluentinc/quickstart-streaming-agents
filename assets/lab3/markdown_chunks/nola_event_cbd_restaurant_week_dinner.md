# CBD Restaurant Week Dinner Service

**Frequency:** Seasonal promotional period
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Location:** Multiple CBD restaurants
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Special prix fixe dinner menus during promotional dining periods. Participating restaurants offer multi-course dinners creating elevated demand across CBD dining scene with food enthusiasts exploring multiple venues.

Transportation demand high throughout dinner hours (5:45 PM - 7:30 PM) with diners from French Quarter, Uptown, and Garden District. Riverboat service popular for reaching waterfront restaurant destinations during special dining events.
