# Steamboat Natchez Brunch Cruise

**Frequency:** Daily departures
**Time:** 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM
**Location:** Toulouse Street Wharf, French Quarter
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Popular riverboat brunch cruise featuring Creole buffet and live jazz band aboard historic paddlewheeler. Attracts 300-400 tourists and locals for scenic Mississippi River experience with dining.

Transportation demand concentrates before boarding (10:30 AM - 11:00 AM) with passengers arriving from CBD, Warehouse District, and Uptown hotels. Creates complementary demand for riverboat taxi services as tourists seek water-based transportation experiences.
