# Marigny Artisan Market

**Frequency:** Weekly Saturday event
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
**Location:** Washington Square Park, Marigny
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Neighborhood artisan market featuring handmade crafts, jewelry, art, and local food vendors. Community-oriented event attracting 200-300 shoppers to the vibrant Marigny neighborhood adjacent to French Quarter.

Transportation demand builds through late morning with peak (11:00 AM - 12:00 PM) as shoppers from French Quarter, Bywater, and downtown areas arrive. Close proximity to French Quarter makes riverboat taxi convenient for tourists combining market visit with broader neighborhood exploration.
