# French Quarter History Walking Lunch

**Frequency:** Daily departures
**Time:** 12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter, multiple restaurant stops
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Combination walking tour and culinary experience featuring French Quarter history with stops at historic restaurants. Groups of 15-20 tourists sample Creole cuisine while learning neighborhood history.

Transportation demand at tour start (11:45 AM - 12:15 PM) with tourists from CBD and Warehouse District hotels. Tours conclude at French Quarter restaurants, creating minimal return demand as participants continue exploring or dining.
