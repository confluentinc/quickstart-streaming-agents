# Warehouse District Wine Tasting

**Frequency:** Weekly Saturday event
**Time:** 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM
**Location:** Warehouse District wine bars and galleries
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Guided wine tasting events at Warehouse District wine bars featuring regional and international selections. Intimate groups of 25-40 wine enthusiasts explore curated tastings in art gallery district setting.

Light transportation demand at event start (12:15 PM - 12:45 PM) with participants from French Quarter and Garden District. Sophisticated clientele appreciates riverboat arrival as complement to refined wine experience.
