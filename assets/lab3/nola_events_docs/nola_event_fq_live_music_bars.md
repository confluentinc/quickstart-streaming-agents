# French Quarter Live Music Bar Scene

**Frequency:** Nightly entertainment
**Time:** 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter music bars and clubs
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Prime evening hours for French Quarter bars featuring live jazz, blues, and funk bands. Attracts 1,000-1,500 music fans nightly to venues throughout district offering authentic New Orleans musical experiences.

Transportation demand builds through evening (7:45 PM - 9:00 PM) with music enthusiasts from CBD, Warehouse District, and surrounding hotels. French Quarter's concentration of live music venues creates sustained demand for riverboat taxi services during peak entertainment hours.
