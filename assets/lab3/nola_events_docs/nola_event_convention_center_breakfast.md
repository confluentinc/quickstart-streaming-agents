# Convention Center Business Breakfast

**Frequency:** Weekly during convention season
**Time:** 7:30 AM - 9:00 AM
**Location:** Ernest N. Morial Convention Center
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Large-scale business breakfast serving 500-1,000 convention attendees, featuring industry presentations and networking. Part of recurring convention and trade show schedule at the massive riverfront facility.

Transportation demand concentrates during arrival (7:15 AM - 7:45 AM) with attendees coming from French Quarter, CBD, and Warehouse District hotels. Riverboat taxi services provide efficient direct access to Convention Center riverfront entrance, avoiding street traffic congestion.
