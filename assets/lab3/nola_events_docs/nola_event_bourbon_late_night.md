# Bourbon Street Late Night Bar Scene

**Frequency:** Nightly entertainment district
**Time:** 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM
**Location:** Bourbon Street, French Quarter
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Peak late-night period on famous Bourbon Street with bars, clubs, and street performers. Attracts 2,000-3,000 revelers nightly to iconic nightlife destination featuring live music, dancing, and party atmosphere.

Transportation demand throughout late evening (10:00 PM - 12:00 AM) with party-goers arriving from hotels across city. Major return demand surge at bar closing (2:00 AM - 3:00 AM) as crowds disperse seeking transportation to hotels and other districts.
