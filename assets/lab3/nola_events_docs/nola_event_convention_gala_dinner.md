# Convention Center Gala Dinner

**Frequency:** Weekly during convention season
**Time:** 6:30 PM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Ernest N. Morial Convention Center
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Large-scale gala dinners serving 500-1,500 convention attendees with keynote speakers and awards. Formal events feature multi-course meals and entertainment in massive riverfront facility.

Transportation demand peaks during arrival (6:15 PM - 6:45 PM) with formally dressed attendees from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Convention Center riverfront location makes riverboat taxi efficient and elegant arrival option for gala events.
