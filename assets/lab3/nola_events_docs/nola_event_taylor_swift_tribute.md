# Taylor Swift Tribute Concert - Fillmore New Orleans

**Frequency:** Recurring touring event
**Time:** 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM
**Location:** The Fillmore New Orleans, 6 Canal Street, CBD
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Sold-out tribute concert featuring "1989 - The Taylor Swift Experience" performing hits spanning Swift's entire catalog for 2,200 enthusiastic fans. The high-energy show includes costume changes, choreography, and fan sing-alongs creating an authentic concert experience.

The event attracts predominantly young adults and families from throughout Louisiana, Mississippi, and Alabama. Many attendees arrive in costume and themed outfits, creating a festival-like atmosphere that extends beyond the venue into surrounding CBD and French Quarter areas.

Transportation demand peaks during arrival (7:00 PM - 8:00 PM) and departure (11:00 PM - 12:30 AM) periods. The downtown location and young demographic create strong demand for safe, reliable transportation options, particularly for groups of friends traveling together from various districts.

Post-concert activities typically continue in the French Quarter as attendees seek late-night dining and entertainment, generating sustained transportation demand throughout the downtown entertainment corridor.
