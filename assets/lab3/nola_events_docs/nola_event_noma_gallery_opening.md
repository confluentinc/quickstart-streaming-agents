# New Orleans Museum of Art Gallery Opening

**Frequency:** Monthly exhibition openings
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 1:00 PM
**Location:** New Orleans Museum of Art, City Park
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Special gallery opening events for NOMA members and art patrons featuring new exhibitions. Light refreshments and curator talks accompany exclusive preview access before public opening.

Light transportation demand during arrival (9:45 AM - 10:30 AM) with art enthusiasts from Garden District, French Quarter, and Uptown. Most attendees are local members, creating minimal overall demand impact despite cultural significance.
