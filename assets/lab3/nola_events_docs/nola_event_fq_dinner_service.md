# French Quarter Prime Dinner Service

**Frequency:** Daily evening service
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter restaurants (widespread)
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Peak dinner period across French Quarter's diverse restaurant scene from casual to upscale establishments. Attracts 2,000-3,000 diners nightly seeking authentic Creole and French cuisine in historic district settings.

Transportation demand concentrated during dinner arrival (5:45 PM - 7:00 PM) with diners from hotels across city seeking French Quarter dining experiences. Restaurant density and evening atmosphere create sustained demand for riverboat taxi services throughout dinner hours.
