# French Quarter Late Night Food Service

**Frequency:** Nightly late-night service
**Time:** 12:00 AM - 4:00 AM
**Location:** 24-hour diners, late-night food trucks, and beignet cafes, French Quarter
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Round-the-clock food service at iconic 24-hour establishments and late-night food trucks throughout French Quarter. Attracts 800-1,200 late-night diners including bar-goers seeking post-nightlife meals, night shift workers, early-rising tourists, and locals craving signature beignets and coffee.

Transportation demand peaks during bar closing aftermath (12:00 AM - 2:00 AM) and early morning breakfast seekers (3:00 AM - 4:00 AM). Late-night food culture creates consistent demand for rides to famous 24-hour cafes from CBD, Marigny, and downtown hotels, with riverboat service providing safe late-night transportation option.
