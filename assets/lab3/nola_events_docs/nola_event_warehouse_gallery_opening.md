# Warehouse District Gallery Opening

**Frequency:** Monthly First Saturday evening
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Location:** Warehouse District galleries
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Art Walk evening with multiple gallery openings featuring wine, artists, and new exhibitions. Attracts 500-700 art enthusiasts, collectors, and culturally-engaged professionals to arts district.

Transportation demand increases throughout early evening (5:45 PM - 6:30 PM) with art patrons from French Quarter, Garden District, and Uptown. Gallery-hopping pattern creates sustained demand as attendees move between venues.
