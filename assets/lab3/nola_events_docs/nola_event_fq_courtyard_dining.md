# French Quarter Courtyard Dining

**Frequency:** Daily lunch and afternoon service
**Time:** 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM
**Location:** Historic French Quarter courtyards
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Leisurely courtyard dining at iconic French Quarter restaurants featuring hidden gardens and historic patios. Attracts 400-600 diners seeking romantic, shaded outdoor dining experiences in authentic Creole settings.

Transportation demand throughout early afternoon (12:00 PM - 2:00 PM) with diners from Garden District, Uptown, and CBD areas. Elegant courtyard dining occasions create preference for sophisticated riverboat arrival complementing refined atmosphere.
