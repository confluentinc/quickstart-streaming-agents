# Warehouse District Club Night

**Frequency:** Weekly Thursday-Saturday
**Time:** 10:00 PM - 2:00 AM
**Location:** Various Warehouse District clubs
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Late-night clubbing scene in Warehouse District featuring multiple venues with DJs and dancing. Attracts 500-800 club-goers to arts district's nightlife venues offering sophisticated late-night entertainment.

Transportation demand increases late evening (10:00 PM - 11:00 PM) with club patrons from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Return demand concentrated at closing (2:00 AM - 2:30 AM) as clubs empty simultaneously.
