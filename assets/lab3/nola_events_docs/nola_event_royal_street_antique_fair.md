# Royal Street Antique Fair - French Quarter

**Frequency:** Recurring monthly event
**Time:** 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
**Location:** Royal Street between St. Louis and Ursulines Streets
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Monthly gathering of 25 established antique dealers and collectors displaying rare furniture, art, jewelry, and collectibles along the historic Royal Street corridor. The fair caters to serious collectors and interior designers seeking authentic period pieces and Louisiana cultural artifacts.

This specialized event draws a focused audience of antique enthusiasts who typically spend several hours browsing and negotiating purchases. The demographic tends to be older, affluent visitors who prefer comfortable transportation options when carrying valuable acquisitions.

Transportation demand remains steady but moderate throughout the day, with gradual arrival patterns between 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM and departure waves around 3:00 PM - 4:00 PM. Many attendees combine the fair with French Quarter dining, extending their area presence beyond the official event hours.

The fair's reputation attracts collectors from Mississippi, Alabama, and Texas who often utilize riverboat taxi services to transport delicate antiques safely to parking areas outside the French Quarter.
