# Uptown Wine Pairing Dinner

**Frequency:** Weekly special events
**Time:** 7:00 PM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Various Uptown restaurants
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Special multi-course wine pairing dinners at upscale Uptown restaurants. Intimate events for 30-50 wine and food enthusiasts featuring sommelier-selected pairings with chef's tasting menus.

Light transportation demand during arrival (6:45 PM - 7:15 PM) with affluent diners from French Quarter and Garden District. Sophisticated clientele appreciates elegant riverboat arrival option for special culinary occasions.
