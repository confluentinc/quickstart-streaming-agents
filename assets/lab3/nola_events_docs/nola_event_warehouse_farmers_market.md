# Warehouse District Farmers Market

**Frequency:** Weekly Saturday morning
**Time:** 7:30 AM - 11:00 AM
**Location:** Warehouse District, Magazine Street
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Popular weekend farmers market featuring local produce, artisan foods, and crafts. Attracts suburban shoppers, food enthusiasts, and families from throughout New Orleans metro area seeking fresh ingredients and unique products.

Transportation demand peaks at market opening (7:30 AM - 8:30 AM) and mid-market (9:30 AM - 10:30 AM). Many attendees from French Quarter, Garden District, and Uptown prefer riverboat service to avoid parking challenges while carrying market purchases.
