# French Quarter Sunset Cocktail Hour

**Frequency:** Daily evening pattern
**Time:** 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter balcony bars and lounges
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Late afternoon cocktail period at French Quarter's famous balcony bars and historic cocktail lounges. Attracts 800-1,200 patrons seeking sunset drinks, people-watching, and classic New Orleans cocktails in iconic settings.

Transportation demand increases through late afternoon (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM) with cocktail enthusiasts from CBD, Warehouse District, and Uptown hotels. Classic French Quarter experience creates strong demand for riverboat arrival at balcony bar destinations.
