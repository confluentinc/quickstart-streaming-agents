# French Quarter Afternoon Shopping Peak

**Frequency:** Daily afternoon pattern
**Time:** 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter shops and boutiques
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Peak afternoon shopping period across French Quarter's antique shops, boutiques, and specialty stores. Attracts 1,000-1,500 shoppers browsing unique goods ranging from antiques to local crafts along Royal, Chartres, and Decatur Streets.

Transportation demand builds through afternoon (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM) with shoppers from CBD hotels, Warehouse District, and Uptown. French Quarter shopping experience creates sustained demand for riverboat taxi services avoiding parking challenges.
