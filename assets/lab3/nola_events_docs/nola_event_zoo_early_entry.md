# Audubon Zoo Early Entry Event

**Frequency:** Monthly member event
**Time:** 8:30 AM - 10:00 AM
**Location:** Audubon Zoo, Uptown
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Special early access for zoo members to experience exhibits before general public opening. Attracts families with young children seeking quieter zoo experience and special feeding demonstrations.

Light transportation demand during arrival (8:15 AM - 8:45 AM) with families from French Quarter and downtown hotels seeking family-friendly riverboat transit to Uptown zoo location. Most attendees are local members using personal vehicles.
