# French Quarter Evening Ghost Tours

**Frequency:** Multiple daily departures
**Time:** 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter, various departure points
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Popular haunted history walking tours exploring French Quarter's supernatural folklore. Multiple tour companies operate simultaneous evening departures accommodating 400-500 tourists nightly seeking spooky entertainment.

Transportation demand peaks at evening tour starts (6:45 PM - 7:15 PM) with tourists arriving from CBD, Warehouse District, and Uptown hotels. Evening timing and French Quarter destination create strong demand for riverboat taxi services.
