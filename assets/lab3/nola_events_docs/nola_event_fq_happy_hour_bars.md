# French Quarter Happy Hour Rush

**Frequency:** Daily weekday and weekend
**Time:** 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
**Location:** French Quarter bars and restaurants
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Peak happy hour period across numerous French Quarter bars offering drink specials and appetizers. Creates concentrated demand with 1,200-1,800 patrons frequenting establishments throughout the district for after-work and pre-dinner socializing.

Transportation demand surges as workday ends (3:00 PM - 5:00 PM) with professionals from CBD offices and tourists from surrounding hotels. French Quarter's reputation for cocktail culture drives sustained riverboat taxi demand during happy hour window.
