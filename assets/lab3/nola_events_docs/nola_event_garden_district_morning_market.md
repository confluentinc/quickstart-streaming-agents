# Garden District Morning Market

**Frequency:** Weekly Sunday morning
**Time:** 8:00 AM - 11:00 AM
**Location:** Lafayette Square, Garden District
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Upscale neighborhood market featuring organic produce, artisanal goods, and prepared foods in historic Garden District setting. Attracts 300-400 affluent shoppers and food enthusiasts seeking premium local products.

Transportation demand steady throughout market hours with peaks at opening (8:00 AM - 8:30 AM) and mid-market (9:30 AM - 10:00 AM). Shoppers from French Quarter and CBD utilize riverboat service to avoid limited street parking while carrying market purchases.
