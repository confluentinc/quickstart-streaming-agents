# CBD Restaurant Week Lunch Service

**Frequency:** Seasonal promotional period
**Time:** 11:30 AM - 2:00 PM
**Location:** Multiple CBD restaurants
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Special prix fixe lunch menus during promotional dining periods. Participating restaurants offer discounted multi-course lunches, creating increased demand across CBD dining scene with food enthusiasts exploring multiple venues.

Transportation demand elevated throughout lunch period (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM) with food enthusiasts from French Quarter, Uptown, and surrounding districts. Riverboat service popular for reaching waterfront restaurant destinations.
