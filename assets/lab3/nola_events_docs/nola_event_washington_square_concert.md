# Washington Square Park Fall Concert - Marigny

**Frequency:** Recurring seasonal event
**Time:** 6:00 PM - 8:30 PM
**Location:** Washington Square Park, Marigny
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Free community concert featuring the New Orleans Recreation Department Concert Band performing seasonal classics and local favorites in the historic Washington Square Park. This family-friendly event serves the Marigny neighborhood and surrounding communities with accessible outdoor entertainment.

The concert attracts local families, elderly residents, and visitors exploring the Marigny district. The park's central location makes it a natural gathering point for neighborhood residents who often walk or bike to the event, though some attendees arrive from other districts.

Transportation demand remains light but steady, with gradual arrival patterns between 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM and departure waves around 8:30 PM - 9:00 PM. Some visitors combine the concert with dinner at nearby Marigny restaurants, extending their presence in the area beyond the performance schedule.

The event's free admission and family orientation attract residents from across New Orleans seeking affordable cultural entertainment, creating modest cross-district transportation needs.
