# Harrah's Casino Evening Peak

**Frequency:** Daily evening pattern
**Time:** 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM
**Location:** Harrah's Casino, CBD riverfront
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Peak evening gaming and entertainment period at major riverfront casino. Attracts 2,000-3,000 visitors nightly for gaming, restaurants, and nightlife entertainment in expansive facility.

Transportation demand concentrated during arrival (6:45 PM - 8:00 PM) with casino guests from French Quarter hotels and surrounding districts. Riverfront location creates natural synergy with riverboat taxi services for convenient casino access.
