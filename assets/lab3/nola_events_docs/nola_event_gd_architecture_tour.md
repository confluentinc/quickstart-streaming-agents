# Garden District Architecture Tour

**Frequency:** Daily departures
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
**Location:** Garden District, Lafayette Square start
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Guided tours showcasing historic mansions, gardens, and Victorian architecture. Attracts architecture enthusiasts, tourists, and design professionals exploring New Orleans' most elegant residential neighborhood.

Transportation demand at tour departure (9:45 AM - 10:15 AM) with participants from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Many tourists combine this tour with other activities, creating return demand to downtown areas around noon.
