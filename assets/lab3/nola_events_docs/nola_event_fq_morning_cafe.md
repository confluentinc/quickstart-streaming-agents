# French Quarter Morning Cafe Rush

**Frequency:** Daily morning pattern
**Time:** 6:30 AM - 9:00 AM
**Location:** Cafe Du Monde and French Quarter cafes
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Morning rush at iconic French Quarter cafes, particularly Cafe Du Monde and Decatur Street coffee houses. Attracts 800-1,200 early risers including locals, tourists, and business travelers seeking beignets, coffee, and breakfast in authentic French Quarter atmosphere.

Transportation demand peaks during morning arrival (6:30 AM - 7:30 AM) with tourists from CBD and Warehouse District hotels seeking classic New Orleans breakfast experience. Riverboat taxi services provide scenic morning commute to French Quarter dining destinations.
