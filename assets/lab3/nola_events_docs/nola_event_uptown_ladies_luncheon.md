# Uptown Charitable Ladies Luncheon

**Frequency:** Monthly social event
**Time:** 12:30 PM - 3:00 PM
**Location:** Various Uptown restaurants (rotating)
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Social luncheon for women's charitable organizations and garden clubs. Intimate gatherings of 40-60 attendees at upscale Uptown dining establishments supporting various philanthropic causes.

Light transportation demand during arrival (12:15 PM - 12:45 PM) with attendees from Garden District and French Quarter areas. Well-dressed participants appreciate elegant riverboat arrival option for special luncheon occasions.
