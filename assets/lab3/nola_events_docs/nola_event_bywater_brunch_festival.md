# Bywater Weekend Brunch Festival

**Frequency:** Monthly Saturday event
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM
**Location:** Bywater neighborhood restaurants (multiple venues)
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Neighborhood brunch crawl featuring participating Bywater restaurants offering special menus, live music, and drink specials. Attracts 400-500 food enthusiasts exploring the artistic neighborhood's culinary scene.

Transportation demand increases throughout morning with peak arrival (10:30 AM - 11:30 AM) as participants from French Quarter, Marigny, and Uptown converge on Bywater. Return demand spreads across afternoon as attendees conclude multi-restaurant experiences.
