# Convention Center Networking Lunch

**Frequency:** Weekly during convention season
**Time:** 12:00 PM - 1:30 PM
**Location:** Ernest N. Morial Convention Center
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Large networking lunch events serving 300-800 convention attendees during major trade shows and conferences. Features buffet dining and structured networking sessions in convention center meeting spaces.

Transportation demand peaks before lunch (11:45 AM - 12:15 PM) with attendees from French Quarter and CBD hotels. Riverfront Convention Center location makes riverboat taxi efficient option for avoiding midday traffic congestion.
