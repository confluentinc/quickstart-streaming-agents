# The Metropolitan Late Night DJ Event

**Frequency:** Weekly Friday and Saturday
**Time:** 11:00 PM - 3:00 AM
**Location:** The Metropolitan, CBD
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Dance club featuring touring and local DJs spinning electronic, house, and hip-hop music. Nightclub attracts 400-600 club-goers for late-night dancing and bottle service in upscale venue.

Transportation demand builds late evening (10:45 PM - 11:30 PM) with club patrons from French Quarter hotels and surrounding areas. Return demand concentrated at closing (3:00 AM - 3:30 AM) as nightclub empties.
