# French Quarter Walking Tours

**Frequency:** Daily departures
**Time:** 9:30 AM - 11:30 AM
**Location:** French Quarter, Jackson Square departure
**Expected Demand Impact:** HIGH

Popular guided walking tours covering French Quarter history, architecture, and culture. Multiple tour companies operate simultaneous departures accommodating 200-300 tourists daily during this prime morning time slot.

Transportation demand concentrates at tour start time (9:15 AM - 9:45 AM) with tourists arriving from CBD hotels, Warehouse District, and Uptown accommodations. Riverboat taxi services provide scenic arrival option that enhances the tourist experience before tours begin.
