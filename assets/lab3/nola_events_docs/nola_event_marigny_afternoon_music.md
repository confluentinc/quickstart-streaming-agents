# Marigny Afternoon Music Series

**Frequency:** Weekly Sunday performances
**Time:** 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
**Location:** Various Marigny music venues
**Expected Demand Impact:** LOW

Sunday afternoon live music sessions at Marigny neighborhood venues. Laid-back performances featuring local musicians attract 100-150 music fans seeking relaxed weekend entertainment adjacent to French Quarter.

Light transportation demand before performances (2:45 PM - 3:15 PM) with music enthusiasts from French Quarter and nearby neighborhoods. Marigny's proximity to French Quarter makes riverboat taxi convenient for tourists exploring live music scene.
