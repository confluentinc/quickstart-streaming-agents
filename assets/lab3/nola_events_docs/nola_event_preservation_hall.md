# Preservation Hall Jazz Performance

**Frequency:** Nightly performances
**Time:** 8:00 PM - 10:00 PM
**Location:** Preservation Hall, French Quarter
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Iconic traditional jazz venue presenting authentic New Orleans jazz in intimate historic hall. Multiple nightly sets attract 300-400 jazz purists and tourists seeking legendary musical experience.

Transportation demand before show times (7:30 PM - 8:00 PM) with music fans from CBD and Uptown areas. Compact venue and French Quarter location create steady evening demand as multiple show times stagger arrivals throughout evening.
