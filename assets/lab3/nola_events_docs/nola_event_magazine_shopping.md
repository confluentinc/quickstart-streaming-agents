# Magazine Street Shopping Peak

**Frequency:** Daily with weekend concentration
**Time:** 12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
**Location:** Magazine Street, Uptown to Garden District
**Expected Demand Impact:** MEDIUM

Peak shopping period along six-mile Magazine Street corridor featuring boutiques, antiques, galleries, and restaurants. Attracts shoppers and browsers throughout afternoon with concentration during lunch and early afternoon.

Transportation demand builds at midday (12:00 PM - 1:00 PM) with shoppers from French Quarter hotels and CBD seeking access to unique retail district. Return demand spreads across late afternoon as shopping excursions conclude.
